About Us

Since its inception in 2017 just with forty students, under the umbrella of Karunya Educational Trust at our own premises situated in Sri. Putrampappa, Karunya First Grade College has reached up to the mark of 600 students within a short span of five years. We at Karunya, try to ensure the best of continuous learning and the overall development of students to meet the needs of individuals who lead the nation of tomorrow. College offers B.Com course as per the Bangalore University syllabus to build competent and initiative professionals.

Karunya First Grade College is committed to developing competent human resources who can fit themselves in the global scenario for excellence and professionalism. With this end, the institution engages in enabling excellence in education for an effective learning environment. We emphasize curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities and have achieved tremendous academic results — 80% of students secured exemplary marks (>85%) and many others gained first class distinction.
